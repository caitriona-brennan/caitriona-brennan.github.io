Tanger Serif

Type Specimen and Website


The brief for this project was to design a type specimen and website for Tanger Serif. I aimed to highlight its unique qualities and selling points, and effectively display the typeface as a whole. The website focuses on providing a full overview of the typeface and its capabilities. The website is interactive and responsive, to ensure that it is usable to intuitive for every user. For the print portion, I focused on the history and influences of the font, and was inspired by the evolution of type.
