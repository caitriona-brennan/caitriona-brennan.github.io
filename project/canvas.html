Dublin Canvas

Mural


Dublin Canvas is a community project run by Dublin County Council, where artists are invited to submit their artwork for selection, and if chosen paint the design onto an electricity box in the city. I had the privilege of painting my design inspired by the seanfhocal 'Go n-Éirí an Bóthar Leat', which translates to 'May the Road Rise to Meet You'.
